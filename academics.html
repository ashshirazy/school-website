Our Academic Structure

Our academic program is divided into three main sections:

  • Nursery & Primary: Foundational learning through fun, reading, writing, and numeracy.
  • Junior Secondary: Introduction to core subjects and development of study skills.
  • Senior Secondary: Preparation for WAEC, NECO, and JAMB examinations with science, arts, and commercial streams.

We also offer ICT training, cultural clubs, debate teams, and sports programs to develop well-rounded students.
