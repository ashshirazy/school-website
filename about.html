About Ash-shirazy Academy

Ash-shirazy Academy was established in 2020 with the mission to provide quality education, promote moral values, and prepare young minds for leadership roles in society.

Our school operates from Nursery to Senior Secondary level, offering a balanced curriculum that blends academics, moral instruction, and extracurricular activities.

Our Mission

To build a community of learners dedicated to achieving excellence in education and character.

Our Vision

To be one of the top educational institutions in Nigeria that nurtures future leaders and innovators.
